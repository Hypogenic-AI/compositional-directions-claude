# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Deep Read)

1. **park2023_linear_representation_hypothesis.pdf**
   - Authors: Kiho Park, Yo Joong Choe, Victor Veitch
   - Year: 2024 (ICML)
   - arXiv: 2311.03658
   - Why relevant: Foundational formalization of the linear representation hypothesis. Defines causal inner product, connects subspace/measurement/intervention notions.

2. **park2024_geometry_categorical_hierarchical_concepts.pdf**
   - Authors: Kiho Park, Yo Joong Choe, Yibo Jiang, Victor Veitch
   - Year: 2025 (ICLR)
   - arXiv: 2406.01506
   - Why relevant: Extends LRH to categorical concepts as polytopes, proves hierarchical orthogonality theorem — key evidence for compositional subspaces.

3. **todd2023_function_vectors.pdf**
   - Authors: Eric Todd, Millicent L. Li, Arnab Sen Sharma, Aaron Mueller, Byron C. Wallace, David Bau
   - Year: 2024 (ICLR)
   - arXiv: 2310.15213
   - Why relevant: Discovers function vectors, tests vector algebra composition of FVs — direct evidence for and against compositionality.

4. **opielka2026_causality_invariance_concept_vectors.pdf**
   - Authors: Gustaw Opiełka, Hannes Rosenbusch, Claire E. Stevenson
   - Year: 2026 (ICLR)
   - arXiv: 2602.22424
   - Why relevant: Shows FVs are NOT format-invariant, introduces Concept Vectors — reveals two types of linear directions with different compositional properties.
